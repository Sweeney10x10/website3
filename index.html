About Me


Hello! My name is Chris Sweeney and I'm a UK-based Financial Data Project Specialist with 20 years experience supporting global banks, investment firms and insurance companies with £100m+ transformation programmes and mission-critical projects.

Originally an accountant, I began my career in finance and investments before transitioning into freelance consultancy in 2007. Since then, I’ve helped multiple organizations harness the power of technology, data, and reporting to drive informed decision-making.

I love solving complex problems and thrive on projects where I can combine my finance background with my passion for coding and analytics.

Clients usually bring me in when they need someone who speaks both finance and tech - someone who understands financial terminology yet can also build the systems, data models, financial models and dashboards to support strategic decisions.

Ultimately, I’m here to transform your data into valuable, actionable insights - helping your finance teams make smarter decisions by turning data into clarity, and clarity into action.
